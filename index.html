CommandX

An intersectional tech event for students.

About the event

Command X is a two-day long conference hosted by Command Tech that focuses on creating a community of leaders through a variety of events such as workshops, panels, and keynotes that promote different technology applications in our current world.

The event will take place virtually on Discord and Zoom on August 10000.

FAQ

What is the conference about?

The main theme is intersectional applications of technology and will feature speakers who work in different areas of the tech industry. There will also be fun games and activities!

Is it free?

Yes, it's 100% free of charge!

Where is the Zoom link?

Sign up for the various events happening during the conference using the links in the section below. The meeting link will be emailed to you and posted on the Discord prior to the scheduled event!
